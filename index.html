Welcome to PowerBlocker

Need to stop living and breathing powerschool? Use PowerBlocker!

Do you find yourself checking powerschool every ten minutes hoping for your grades to go up? Stop stressing over your grades so much and use this chrome extension. PowerBlocker directs you to our humurous and stress-relieving site to help you stop worrying about your grades to the point where you don't even go on any other website, only PowerSchool.

What is PowerSchool?

PowerSchool is an online website that many school across the country use in order to connect students and parents to their grades. PowerSchool allows students and parents to read teacher comments and keep track of their grades.
