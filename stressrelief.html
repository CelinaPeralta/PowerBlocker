Stress-Relieving Ideas

1. Exercise! Go running or do yoga, sometimes it helps people, sometimes it doesn't.

2. Try meditating! Meditation can decrease anxiety and alter the brain's neural passages, making one more resilient against stress.

3. Breathe. Take some time and breathe. Relax and loosen your body and slow down your heart rate.

4. Talk to people. Confide in your friends and relieve your burdens. Sometimes speaking about your problems can help you relieve them and relieve some of your stress.
