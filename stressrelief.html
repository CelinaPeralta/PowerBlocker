Welcome to Relaxation

Dealing with schoolwork is hard, and we get that. Which is why we've created this website to help you take your mind off of grades and relax. Scroll through our library of soothing gifs or listen to some calming music to help you unwind. But most of all, enjoy yourself.

Here are some quick tips for relieving stress:

1. Yell "Haneul" loudly in a soothing and motherly way.

2. Take long, deep breaths, and as you exhale, loosen your muscles. Repeat this until you feel loose and relaxed.

3. Try to visualize a peaceful, happy scene like the beach or surround yourself with images of nature.

4. Listen to music! Lift you spirits with some uplifiting music, or feel calm with some soothing instrumental music.

These are only four simple tips to relaxation. If you want to take your mind off grades and work for a bit, visit the separate tabs we have included for you. The "calming" tab includes looping gifs for you to watch. There's a calming playlist consisting of instrumental songs that allow you to just drift off in mindless wonder. The "stress relief" tab consists of games and links to different sites for you to play around with. The "laughter" tab includes things for you to laugh at and smile about.
